Our principles of front-end
development

Pixel-perfect and Adaptability

Our front-end developers make layouts exactly according to mock-up. Stores load quickly on all popular devices: mobile phones, tablets, desktop.

Focused on Clients

We follow the best usability practices for online stores and marketplaces. Your customers will want to shop again and again.

Cross-browser and High Performance

Lazy loading and code-splitting layout. This is how we achieve high site loading speed. We test your online store or marketplace so that the pages display equally well on every browser.

Flexibility and Scalability

We use technologies that allow us to make changes to the stores in the shortest possible time. We build a product architecture with the ability to scale up.
